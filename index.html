Situation Report

In the dying years of the 41st Millennium, the Bastior Sub-Sector faces its most terrible trial. What began as scattered vanguard organisms has swelled into a full tendril of Hive Fleet Leviathan. Three agri-worlds have already fallen silent, their biomass rendered down to feed the ever-hungry fleet.

The Imperial forces — Space Marines, Astra Militarum regiments, and the valiant forces of the Adeptus Mechanicus — have established a defence cordon centred on the forge world of Bastior Prime. But the Tyranid advance is relentless, guided by a synaptic intelligence that seems to learn from every Imperial stratagem.

"There is no negotiation with the Great Devourer. There is only the choice between dying quickly and dying slowly. We choose neither — we choose to hold." — Lord Commander Valdris Hemmont, Bastior Defence Cordon
