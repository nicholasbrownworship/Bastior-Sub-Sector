Situation Report — 999.M41

In the dying years of the 41st Millennium, the Bastior Sub-Sector faces its most terrible trial. What began as scattered vanguard organisms has swelled into a full tendril of Hive Fleet Leviathan — approaching from an angle that Imperial strategists failed to guard. Three worlds have fallen silent, their populations dissolved and their biomass devoured.

The forge world of Bastior Prime anchors the defence cordon, its manufactoria hammering out weapons even as the skies fill with bio-ships. Space Marines, Astra Militarum regiments, and the Skitarii of the Mechanicus hold the line — but the Tyranid advance is relentless, guided by a synaptic cunning that learns from every Imperial stratagem employed against it.

There is no negotiation with the Great Devourer. There is only the choice between dying quickly and dying slowly. We choose neither — we choose to hold. — Lord Commander Valdris Hemmont, Bastior Defence Cordon
