Tyrannic War Campaign Rules — Force Modifiers

The Devourer's Hunger

Tyranid forces in this campaign gain the following special rules:

  • Endless Swarm: When a Tyranid unit is destroyed, on a roll of 5+ it returns with D3 models at the start of the following battle round.
  • Synaptic Adaptation: After each battle, the Tyranid player may swap one unit's Crusade Boon for one from the Adaptive Physiology table.
  • Biomass Harvest: At the end of each battle, the Tyranid player gains +1 CP for each unit they destroyed (max +3).

The Emperor's Shield

Imperial forces gain the following special rules:

  • Defenders of Humanity: Imperial units gain +1 to Battle-shock tests when within 6" of an objective marker.
  • Last Stand: Once per battle, when an Imperial unit would be destroyed, roll a D6. On a 6, it survives with 1 model remaining.
  • Crusade of Faith: Imperial forces earn +1 CP whenever they complete a narrative agenda.

The Shadow in the Warp

⬡
While Tyranid forces are present on the battlefield, all Psychic abilities used by non-Tyranid units suffer a -1 to their roll. Units within 12" of a Tyranid unit with the Shadow in the Warp ability cannot use Stratagems that cost 3+ CP. This rule is suspended for units with the Survivors of the Shadow battle honour.
