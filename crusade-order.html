Campaign Special Rules

🧬 Tyranid Faction Rules

Endless Swarm

When a Tyranid unit with 5+ models is destroyed, roll a D6. On a 5+, the unit returns at the start of the next battle round with D3 models, placed within 6" of a table edge.

Synaptic Adaptation

After each battle, the Tyranid player may swap one unit's Crusade Boon for a different one from the Adaptive Physiology list (see Rules Reference).

Biomass Harvest

At the end of each battle, the Tyranid player gains +1 CP for each enemy unit completely destroyed during the battle, to a maximum of +3 CP.

⚜ Imperial Faction Rules

Defenders of Humanity

Imperial units gain +1 to Battle-shock tests while within 6" of an objective marker. This stacks with other modifiers.

Last Stand

Once per battle, when an Imperial unit would be destroyed, roll a D6. On a 6, that unit instead survives with 1 model remaining at 1 Wound. May not be used by Titanic units.

Crusade of Faith

Imperial forces earn +1 bonus CP (not added to roster total) whenever they complete a narrative Agenda in full. This bonus may be spent immediately on Stratagems.
