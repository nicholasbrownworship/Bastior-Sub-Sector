Crusade Agendas

The campaign agendas for the Tyrannic War Crusade supplement, both universal and faction-specific.

SHADOW IN THE WARP DETECTED — ASTROPATHIC CHOIR DISRUPTED

Each battle, players choose one or more Agendas to pursue. Completing an Agenda earns Experience Points for your units and contributes to your Crusade Points total. The Tyrannic War supplement introduces new Agendas reflecting the desperate nature of this campaign.

Universal Agendas

Universal +2 XP
Slay the Warlord

Destroy the enemy warlord. One of the oldest imperatives of war — cut off the head and the body dies.

Complete: Destroy the enemy Warlord.
Reward: The unit that destroyed the Warlord gains 2 XP.
Universal +1 XP each
Battlefield Supremacy

Hold more objective markers than your opponent at the end of each battle round.

Complete: Control more objectives at round end.
Reward: Each unit that held an objective gains 1 XP.
Universal +3 XP
No Prisoners

The ancient Tyranid menace must be annihilated utterly. Leave no creature standing.

Complete: Destroy 50%+ of the enemy force.
Reward: Your most destructive unit gains 3 XP.
Universal +2 XP
Advance and Secure

Push forward, seize ground, and hold it. Every metre reclaimed is a blow against the Devourer.

Complete: End battle with a unit in enemy deployment zone.
Reward: That unit gains 2 XP.
✦

Tyrannic War Agendas

These agendas are available to all forces in the Bastior Crusade, representing the unique challenges of fighting the Great Devourer.

Tyrannic War +3 XP
Slayer of Monsters

The great bio-constructs of the Hive Mind must be brought low. Prove you can fell the beasts.

Complete: Destroy 1+ Monster or Vehicle unit.
Reward: The unit earns 3 XP and the Slayer of Monsters Battle Honour (re-roll wound rolls of 1 vs Monsters/Vehicles).
Tyrannic War +3 XP
Survivors of the Shadow

Fight through the Shadow in the Warp and emerge with mind and purpose intact — a remarkable feat.

Complete: A Psyker unit successfully manifests 2+ powers while affected by Shadow in the Warp.
Reward: That unit earns the Survivors of the Shadow honour — immune to Shadow in the Warp.
Tyrannic War +2 XP
Purge the Infiltrators

Lictors, Genestealers, and Broodlords have seeded themselves throughout the populace. Hunt them down.

Complete: Destroy all enemy units with the Scout or Infiltrators ability.
Reward: Each unit that destroyed such an enemy gains 2 XP.
Tyrannic War +4 XP
Decapitate the Synapse

Destroy the synaptic nodes that hold the swarm together — without guidance, the lesser creatures falter.

Complete: Destroy all enemy units with the Synapse keyword.
Reward: Earn 4 XP split between surviving units, plus 1 bonus Crusade Point.
Tyrannic War +2 XP
Hold the Cordon

The defence lines must not break. Not one step back — every position held buys time for evacuation.

Complete: Do not lose any objective in your deployment zone during the battle.
Reward: All units in your deployment zone at battle end gain 2 XP.
Tyrannic War Special
Sacrifice of the Righteous

Sometimes victory demands the ultimate price. Honour those who give everything so others may live.

Complete: A destroyed unit had 3+ battle honours.
Reward: Their legacy lives on — all friendly units gain 1 CP as tribute. The fallen unit's highest honour is passed to a surviving unit.
✦

Faction Agendas

Space Marines
Death Before Dishonour

A Space Marine unit that charges a unit 3x their number and survives gains triple XP from any kills that engagement.

Astra Militarum
Weight of Numbers

For each full unit of 10+ models that survives the battle, earn 1 bonus XP distributed across your forces.

Tyranids
Consume and Adapt

After destroying an enemy character, roll D6: on 3+ the Hive Mind incorporates their biological data. Gain 2 XP on a unit of choice.

Adeptus Mechanicus
Recover and Repurpose

For each enemy Monster destroyed, the Mechanicus player may make a free Requisition roll after the battle.

Adepta Sororitas
The Emperor's Wrath

If a Miracles of Faith ability is used to save a unit that would otherwise be destroyed, that unit gains the Blessed by Faith battle scar that functions as a boon instead.

Genestealer Cults
From the Shadows

If a unit uses Ambush to arrive from reserve and destroys an enemy unit in the same turn, that unit gains triple XP for that kill.
