Campaign Objectives — Liber Crusadium

Agendas

Choose one or two Agendas before each battle. Completing them earns XP for your units and advances the campaign.

✦
SHADOW IN THE WARP DETECTED — ASTROPATHIC CHOIR DISRUPTED
📋
Rules summary: Before each battle, both players secretly choose 1–2 Agendas from their faction list or the Universal list. Reveal after deployment. If an Agenda is completed, award XP as noted. Some Agendas also award Battle Honours.

Universal Agendas

Universal
Slay the Warlord

Destroy the enemy warlord. Cut off the head and the body dies.

Complete: Destroy the enemy Warlord before the end of the battle.
XP Reward: The unit that dealt the killing blow gains +2 XP.
Universal
Battlefield Supremacy

Hold more objective markers than your opponent at the end of each battle round.

Complete: Control more objectives than your opponent at the end of Round 3+.
XP Reward: Each unit holding an objective gains +1 XP.
Universal
No Prisoners

Annihilate the enemy. Leave nothing standing. The sub-sector requires examples.

Complete: Destroy 50% or more of the enemy force by the battle's end.
XP Reward: Your most destructive unit gains +3 XP.
Universal
Advance and Secure

Push forward and seize enemy ground. Every metre reclaimed is a victory for the Imperium.

Complete: End the battle with at least one unit wholly within the enemy deployment zone.
XP Reward: That unit gains +2 XP.

Tyrannic War Agendas

Tyrannic War
Slayer of Monsters

Bring the great bio-constructs low. Prove you can kill gods made of meat and hunger.

Complete: Destroy at least one Monster or Vehicle unit.
XP Reward: +3 XP. The unit also earns the Slayer of Monsters Battle Honour (re-roll wound rolls of 1 vs Monsters & Vehicles, permanent).
Tyrannic War
Survivors of the Shadow

Fight through the oppressive Shadow in the Warp and emerge with mind and will intact.

Complete: A Psyker unit successfully manifests 2+ psychic powers while affected by Shadow in the Warp.
XP Reward: +3 XP. That unit becomes permanently immune to Shadow in the Warp.
Tyrannic War
Purge the Infiltrators

Hunt down the Lictors, Broodlords, and vanguard organisms seeded throughout the populace before they can summon the swarm.

Complete: Destroy all enemy units with the Scout or Infiltrators ability.
XP Reward: Each unit that destroyed a qualifying enemy gains +2 XP.
Tyrannic War
Decapitate the Synapse

Destroy the synaptic web. Without guidance, the lesser organisms become little more than animals — dangerous, but directionless.

Complete: Destroy every unit with the Synapse keyword currently on the battlefield.
XP Reward: +4 XP split among surviving units + +1 bonus CP.
Tyrannic War
Hold the Cordon

The defence lines must not break. Not one step back. The cost of failure is the entire sub-sector.

Complete: Do not lose control of any objective marker in your deployment zone.
XP Reward: All units in your deployment zone at battle's end gain +2 XP.
Special — Imperial
Sacrifice of the Righteous

Some victories demand the ultimate price. Let their sacrifice mean something.

Complete: A unit with 3+ Battle Honours is destroyed during the battle.
Reward: All friendly units gain +1 CP (usable this battle). That unit's highest honour passes to a surviving unit of your choice.
