Rules Reference

A quick-reference guide to the Tyrannic War Crusade special rules, post-battle sequence, and campaign mechanics.

SHADOW IN THE WARP DETECTED — ASTROPATHIC CHOIR DISRUPTED

Tyrannic War Special Rules

Shadow in the Warp
Tyranid Special Rule — Passive Aura

While a friendly unit with this ability is on the battlefield, all enemy units that are not Chaos or Tyranids suffer the following effects:

  • Each time a Psychic ability is used by an enemy unit within 18" of any friendly unit with this ability, subtract 1 from the Psychic test.
  • Subtract 1 from the Battle-shock test of all enemy units on the battlefield.
  • Enemy units cannot use Stratagems that cost 3+ CP while within 12" of any friendly unit with this ability.

Note: Units with the Survivors of the Shadow battle honour are immune to all effects of Shadow in the Warp.

Synaptic Link
Tyranid Special Rule — Synapse Keyword

Tyranid units within 12" of a friendly unit with the Synapse keyword gain the following benefits:

  • Automatically pass Battle-shock tests.
  • Do not suffer the penalties of Battle-shock while within range.
  • Re-roll charge rolls of 1 when within range of a Synapse unit.

Without Synapse nearby, Tyranid units become unpredictable — they may still fight, but the Hive Mind's iron control slips.

Instinctive Behaviour
Tyranid Special Rule — Out of Synapse

If a Tyranid unit without the Synapse keyword is not within 12" of a friendly Synapse unit at the start of its Movement phase, roll D6:

Post-Battle Sequence

After every battle in the Bastior Campaign, complete the following steps in order:

1
Mark Casualties
For each unit that was destroyed, roll on the Battle Scar table. Record any scars earned. Note: units with Survivors of the Shadow re-roll results of 6 on this table.
2
Award XP
Award XP as follows:
  • +1 XP to all units that participated in the battle
  • +1 XP to all units that completed an Agenda task
  • +2 XP to units that destroyed an enemy unit
  • +3 XP to the unit that destroyed the enemy Warlord
3
Gain Battle Honours
For each unit that increased in rank, consult the Battle Honours table and award the appropriate honour. The player may choose from the options available to the new rank.
4
Earn Crusade Points
The winner earns Crusade Points as follows:
  • +3 CP for winning the battle
  • +1 CP for each completed Agenda (max 2)
  • +1 CP for destroying the enemy Warlord
The loser earns: +1 CP (consolation) +1 CP for each completed Agenda (max 1)
5
Make Requisitions
Spend Crusade Points on Requisitions. Update your Order of Battle, Supply Used, and Crusade Point totals. Record everything in the Battle Log.
6
Update the Campaign Map
Record the battle result on the Sector Map. If a narrative event was triggered, consult the Campaign Event table and apply the result to the affected system.
✦
