Core Special Rules

🧬 Shadow in the Warp

Passive Aura — all Tyranid Synapse units

While any friendly Synapse unit is on the battlefield, all enemy non-Tyranid units are affected by the following:

  • Subtract 1 from Psychic tests made within 18" of any friendly Synapse unit.
  • Subtract 1 from Battle-shock tests for all enemy units on the battlefield.
  • Enemy units cannot use Stratagems costing 3 or more CP while within 12" of a friendly Synapse unit.
⬡
Units with the Survivors of the Shadow Battle Honour are completely immune to all effects of Shadow in the Warp.
🔗 Synaptic Link

Tyranid units within 12" of a friendly Synapse unit

  • Automatically pass all Battle-shock tests.
  • Do not suffer any penalties associated with Battle-shock while in Synapse range.
  • Re-roll unmodified charge roll results of 1 while within Synapse range.
⚙ Adaptive Physiology Table — D6

After each battle, the Tyranid player may roll once on this table for one surviving unit. The result replaces any existing Crusade Boon from this table.

Post-Battle Sequence (in order)

  1. 01 Determine the result. Check who won, drew, or lost. Award CP accordingly: +3 for a victory, +1 for a draw.
  2. 02 Score Agendas. Reveal chosen Agendas and check completion. Award XP to qualifying units. Record on roster.
  3. 03 Battle Scars. For each unit destroyed during the battle, roll D6 on the Battle Scar table. Apply results immediately.
  4. 04 Promotions & Honours. Check if any unit has crossed a rank threshold. Award Battle Honours for newly Legendary units.
  5. 05 Tyranid Adaptation. The Tyranid player may roll on the Adaptive Physiology table for one surviving unit.
  6. 06 Update records. Log the battle in the Battle Log, update the Roster, adjust supply totals, and spend Requisitions as desired.
