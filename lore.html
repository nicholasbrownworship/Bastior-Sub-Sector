The Bastior Sub-Sector

Located in the trailing reaches of Segmentum Ultima, the Bastior Sub-Sector comprises seven inhabited star systems bound together by ancient pacts of mutual defence and trade. For millennia it has been a prosperous backwater — its forge world producing reliable materiel, its agri-worlds feeding Crusade fleets bound for the Eastern Fringe.

That prosperity ended when the first hive ships were detected crossing the Mandeville point of the Calyx System. Within months, what Imperial intelligence dismissed as a minor tendril had revealed itself as a full feeding tongue of Hive Fleet Leviathan, guided with terrible cunning from below rather than above.

"We have fought the sons of Chaos, the eternal undead, and the arrogant Aeldari — and prevailed. But this is different. This enemy does not want our worlds. It wants to unmake them." — Canoness Superior Yvelline Rast, Order of the Crimson Shroud
✦

The Seven Systems

The sub-sector encompasses seven inhabited systems, each playing a vital strategic role in the Imperium's efforts to contain the Tyranid advance.

Forge World
Bastior Prime

The forge world and administrative capital of the sub-sector. Its manufactoria still hammer out weapons day and night, and its Skitarii legions defend the cordon with cold mechanical dedication. Currently besieged but holding.

FALLEN — Consumed
Calyx Secundus

Once a thriving agri-world of four billion souls. Now a stripped husk, its topsoil digested and its atmosphere fouled with Tyranid spores. The site of the first confirmed planetary consumption in the sub-sector.

Hive World
Morthix IV

A teeming hive world of eleven billion. Its underhives have been infiltrated by Genestealer cults, revealed only when the Tyranid vanguard arrived and the sleepers awoke. Intense street-by-street fighting continues.

FALLEN — Contested
Ostria Reach

A mid-size civilised world that was the first to fall to direct assault. The last transmission from Governor Serevant was received 47 days into the campaign. Kill-teams have since confirmed the world is fully consumed.

Under Siege
Vethanus Station

A vital void station and naval yard. Its strategic importance cannot be overstated — if it falls, the Imperial Navy loses its primary repair and resupply point in the sub-sector. Currently under constant Tyranid bio-ship pressure.

Staging Ground
Pentarch

A cold, sparsely populated mining world repurposed as a major Imperial staging ground and refugee processing centre. Its deep bunkers shelter millions of displaced persons from consumed worlds.

Infiltrated
Rho-Kethis

A cemetery world and shrine planet, sacred to the Ecclesiarchy. Thought safe due to its lack of biomass, it has become a staging point for Lictor and Genestealer incursions. The Sisters of Battle have been dispatched.

The Tyranid Threat

Hive Fleet Leviathan — Splinter Tendril Omicron

Unlike the main body of Hive Fleet Leviathan that attacked from below the galactic plane, Splinter Tendril Omicron approaches from the galactic south-east — an angle that Imperial strategists failed to adequately guard. The tendril is estimated to comprise some forty-seven capital hive ships and hundreds of smaller bio-vessels.

What makes Omicron particularly dangerous is its apparent tactical intelligence. Rather than the headlong consumption typical of Tyranid advances, this tendril probes, feints, and isolates — seeming to learn from each engagement and adapting its bio-forms accordingly. Magos Biologis Evander Kael has theorised that a Norn-Queen of exceptional cognitive development may be directing the tendril.

SHADOW IN THE WARP: Astropathic communication has been severely degraded across the sub-sector. Messages to Segmentum Command are taking weeks to arrive, if they arrive at all. The sub-sector must fight its battle largely alone.

Imperial Forces

The defence of the Bastior Sub-Sector has drawn forces from across the Imperium. Space Marine Chapters have answered the call, Astra Militarum regiments have been conscripted and redeployed, and the Adeptus Mechanicus fights to protect its forge world at all costs.

  • Iron Wardens Chapter (Space Marines) — Third Company deployed in full. Known for their siege expertise, they hold the outer defence lines of Bastior Prime.
  • Bastior 1st through 9th Infantry Regiments — Raised from the forge world itself, these warriors know the industrial terrain better than any outsider.
  • Order of the Crimson Shroud (Adepta Sororitas) — Two full Orders Militant deployed to Rho-Kethis and Morthix IV to combat Genestealer cult infiltration.
  • Skitarii Legio Bastior — The forge world's own augmented warrior servants. Over thirty maniples in active deployment.
  • Battlegroup Severance (Imperial Navy) — A reduced fleet centred on the battlecruiser Inexorable Fury, protecting Vethanus Station.
